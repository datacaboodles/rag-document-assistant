
Vending Machine Warranty Policy

Standard Coverage:
- 12-month warranty from date of installation.
- Covers manufacturing defects in parts and labour.
- Free service visits for covered repairs within 48 hours.

What Is NOT Covered:
- Physical damage due to misuse or accidents.
- Water damage caused by improper cleaning procedures.
- Damage from unauthorised third-party repairs.
- Power surge damage.

How to Make a Warranty Claim:
1. Note the machine serial number (found on the back panel).
2. Call our service line or email service@saivending.com.
3. Describe the issue clearly with any error codes displayed.
4. A technician will be scheduled within 48 working hours.
