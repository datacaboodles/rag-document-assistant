
Customer Retention Playbook

Identifying At-Risk Customers:
A customer is at-risk if ANY of the following is true:
- No orders placed in the last 30 days.
- 3 or more complaints filed in the current month.
- Order frequency dropped by more than 40% vs previous quarter.

Retention Actions:

HIGH RISK:
- Personal call from account manager within 24 hours.
- Offer complimentary preventive maintenance visit.
- Apply 15% discount on next 2 orders automatically.

MEDIUM RISK:
- Send personalised message acknowledging the gap.
- Offer a free maintenance check within 7 days.

LOW RISK:
- Include in monthly newsletter.
- Request a review or NPS score.

Upsell Opportunities:
- Customers ordering more than 50 units per month: recommend premium blends.
- Customers with 1 machine: offer second machine at 20% discount.
