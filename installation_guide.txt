
Machine Installation Guide

Pre-Installation Requirements:
- Dedicated 15-amp electrical socket within 1 metre of placement.
- Flat, stable surface capable of supporting 45kg.
- Minimum 30cm clearance on all sides for ventilation.
- Access to clean water supply within 2 metres.

Installation Steps:
1. Unbox machine and remove all protective packaging.
2. Place machine on designated surface.
3. Connect water supply line to rear inlet valve.
4. Plug into dedicated power socket — do not use extension cords.
5. Power on and follow on-screen setup wizard.
6. Run 3 test cycles before serving customers.

Post-Installation Checklist:
- Register machine serial number at saivending.com/register within 7 days.
- Schedule first maintenance visit within 30 days of installation.
- Train at least 2 staff members on daily cleaning procedure.

Serial Number Location:
Found on a sticker on the back panel, format: SV-XXXX-YYYY.
